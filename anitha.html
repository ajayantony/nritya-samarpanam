Anitha Valakuzhy

A Journey of Grace and Devotion

✦

Anitha discovered her love for dance at a young age, growing up in India performing in various cultural events. Her early years were shaped more by a love for performing than by formal, structured training. Her passion for dance remained constant, quietly accompanying her through different phases of life.

After moving to the United States, Anitha felt a strong desire to reconnect with her passion for dance. In 2019, she began her formal training in Bharatanatyam under the guidance of Guru Parvatha Chidambaram in Rochester, NY. What started as a return to a long-held passion soon evolved into a deeply meaningful journey of learning and artistic growth.

Anitha is a devoted mother to her two daughters, Zoey (12) and Zara (7), and shares a loving and supportive partnership with her husband, Ajay. Professionally, she is a computer science engineer. Alongside her family life and career, she has always nurtured a strong inclination toward creativity and artistic expression.

Her Arangetram marks a significant milestone in her artistic journey, not just as a dancer, but as an individual who chose to pursue a lifelong passion alongside everyday responsibilities. It stands as a celebration of dedication, perseverance, and the unwavering support of her family, friends, and Guru.
