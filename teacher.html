Our Guru

Teacher · Mentor · Artist

Guru Lakshmi Devi

Bharatanatyam Exponent · Kalakshetra Tradition

✦

Guru Lakshmi Devi is a celebrated Bharatanatyam exponent whose journey with classical dance spans more than four decades. Trained under the legendary masters of the Kalakshetra tradition in Chennai, she has devoted her life to preserving and propagating the pristine idiom of this ancient art form.

Her own Arangetram, performed at the age of sixteen, marked the beginning of a distinguished career on stages across India and abroad. She has performed at prestigious venues including the Music Academy in Chennai, Bharat Kalachar, and numerous cultural festivals in the United States, United Kingdom, and Singapore.

After years as a performer, Guru Lakshmi Devi turned her focus to teaching, founding her dance school with the mission of nurturing young dancers with the same rigor, discipline, and love for the art that shaped her own journey. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes not only technical excellence but also a deep understanding of the spiritual and devotional dimensions of Bharatanatyam.

Anitha and Sreyoshi represent the finest flowering of her decades of dedicated instruction. Watching them take the stage for their Arangetram fills her with immense pride and joy — a moment she calls the truest reward of a teacher's life.
