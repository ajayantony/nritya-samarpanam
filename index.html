Sreyoshi Sur

✦

A scientist and classical dancer, Sreyoshi began Bharatanatyam at age four in Kolkata and holds a Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of Rochester. Dance has remained her refuge and joy through a distinguished career in biotechnology.
